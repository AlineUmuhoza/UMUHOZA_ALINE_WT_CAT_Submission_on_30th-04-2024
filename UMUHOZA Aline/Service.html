OUR SERVICES

At CES BANK, we offer a comprehensive range of financial services designed to meet your needs and help you achieve your goals. Whether you're saving for the future, purchasing a home, planning for retirement, or growing your wealth, we're here to support you every step of the way. Explore our services below:

1. Savings Accounts

High-Yield Savings Accounts: Earn competitive interest rates while maintaining easy access to your funds.

Youth Savings Accounts: Teach children the importance of saving with our specialized youth savings accounts.

Certificate of Deposit (CD): Lock in a fixed interest rate with our range of CD options.

2. Checking Accounts

Basic Checking: Enjoy no monthly maintenance fees and unlimited transactions with our basic checking account.

Interest-Bearing Checking: Earn interest on your checking account balance with our interest-bearing checking options.

Student Checking: Tailored specifically for students, our student checking accounts offer convenient features and benefits.

3. Loans and Credit

Mortgage Loans: Finance your dream home with our competitive mortgage loan options.

Personal Loans: Access funds for various personal expenses, such as home improvements or unexpected emergencies.

Auto Loans: Get behind the wheel with our flexible auto loan terms and competitive rates.

Business Loans: Support your business growth with our range of business financing solutions.

4. Investments and Wealth Management

Investment Advisory Services: Receive personalized investment advice from our team of experienced financial advisors.

Retirement Planning: Plan for a secure retirement with our comprehensive retirement planning services.

Wealth Management: Grow and protect your wealth with our customized wealth management solutions.

5. Online and Mobile Banking

Online Banking: Manage your accounts, transfer funds, and pay bills conveniently from your computer or mobile device.

Mobile Banking App: Stay connected to your finances on the go with our secure mobile banking app.

Bill Pay: Simplify your finances by scheduling and paying bills online with our bill pay feature

6. Financial Planning and Education

Financial Planning Services: Work with our certified financial planners to create a personalized financial plan tailored to your goals and circumstances.

Financial Education Resources: Access a wealth of educational resources, articles, and tools to enhance your financial literacy and make informed decisions.

7. Business Banking Solutions

Business Checking: Streamline your business finances with our business checking accounts tailored to meet your business needs.

Merchant Services: Accept payments and manage your business transactions efficiently with our merchant services solutions.

Business Credit: Access credit and financing options designed to support your business growth and expansion.
